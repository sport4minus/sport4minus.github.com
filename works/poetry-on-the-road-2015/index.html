Poetry On The Road (2015)

Generative key visual for a poetry festival in Bremen, Germany.

Audio Files of readings have been analysed computationally , mimicking the way algorithms try to figure out emotional expressions in natural language. Variations in pitch, rhythm and volume were translated into colors and directions, used to create a diverse range of visual elements.

 A collaboration between SYNTOP and one/one, who used the output for the creation of beautiful posters, programme leaflets and other media for the festival.
