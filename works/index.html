Works

A selection of free and commissioned works from the fields of art and design, some were solo projects, some were collaborations.

They are interactive or at least autoactive pieces which are brought to life through programming computers or microcontrollers, decontextualization and hacking of hardware, sending and receiveing data, spatial arrangement and some typography and graphic design.

If they are free projects, they most likely deal with emerging technologies and their impact on society and communication. If they are commissioned, they deal with what has to be dealt with.

Enjoy and feel free to ask me about them.
