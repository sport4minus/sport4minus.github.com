Science Express (2009)

Working for Archimedes Exhibitions on their massive mobile exhibition train “Science Express” i had my hands on several interactive exhibits dealing with lots of computational design tasks.

most noteable are the beautiful split-flap display in the first wagon which welcomes the visitor with a mesmerizing choreography of questions and a multitouch table, on which web services are floating around in a very organic manner, waiting to be caught and explored.
