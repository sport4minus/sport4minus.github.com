Esprit HK Mediafacade (2009)

In collaboration with: Art+Com

Generative animations for the Esprit Flagship Store’s Media LED-Facade.

Three modules generate realtime visuals out of recent fashion photos, text files and simply line patterns.

The visuals are finetuned to work best with the extreme aspect ratio of the LED Facade spanning three sides of the building, emphasizing the horizontal lines and the architecture
