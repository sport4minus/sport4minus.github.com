Siemens MedMuseum (2013)

Interactive Exhibits and Media Guide

Two large-scale multimedia exhibits and a location aware media guide for the newly created Museum for history of medical technology in Erlangen - Siemens MedMuseum.

At SYNTOP, we dealt with the interaction and UI design for these elements, from first drafts to final development, including a customized indoor positioning system for the media guide.
