Poetry On The Road (2014)

Generative key visual for a poetry festival in Bremen, Germany.

Poems from the festival programme have been anonymusly analyzed for emotions via an online questionnaire and Amazon Mechanical Turk, a crowdsourcing service, the results are used to generate geometric shapes utilizing Plutchik’s Wheel of Emotions

 A collaboration between SYNTOP and one/one, who used the output for the creation of beautiful posters, programme leaflets and other media for the festival.
