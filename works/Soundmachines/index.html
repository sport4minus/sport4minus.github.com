Soundmachines (2011)

Designed and Developed at TheProduct

The Soundmachines are an instrument for performing electronic music.

Three units, which are resembling standard record players, translate concentric visual patterns into control signals for further processing in any music software.

The rotation of the discs, each holding three tracks, can be synced to a sequencer.

The Soundmachines premiered on the IAA Motor Show in Frankfurt in 2011
